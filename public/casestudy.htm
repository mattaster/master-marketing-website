Section 1: The Hidden Cost of Variety

Why is clinical accessory spend so difficult to control? The answer lies in fragmentation. Unlike capital equipment, accessories are often purchased via P-Cards or departmental budgets ("Rogue Spend"), bypassing central supply chain scrutiny. This leads to SKU proliferation, where a single hospital system might stock dozens of functionally identical cables.
